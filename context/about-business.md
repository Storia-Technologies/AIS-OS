# About Storia Technologies

Storia helps businesses identify where AI can save the most time or money, then builds and implements it — from a focused 10-hour advisory sprint ($2,995) through to ongoing retainers where Storia acts as an embedded AI team. Storia also builds custom software (apps, integrations, automation) when the problem calls for it.

**ICP:** Professional services businesses and owner-operators, typically 5–50 people. Already running well, losing hours every week to manual processes they suspect could be automated. Know AI is relevant but lack internal capability to act on it. Not looking for hype — want someone to show them what's actually possible and build it.

**Revenue model:** Xero is source of truth for invoicing/payments. Stripe for online payments (hosting subscriptions at $249/month, potentially workshop registrations). Most client project invoices paid via direct EFT, reconciled in Xero.
